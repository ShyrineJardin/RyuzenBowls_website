Ryuzen Bowls

Authentic Japanese Ramen

Serving Authentic Japanese Ramen, Crafted with Tradition, Flavor, and Heart in Every Bowl

Ryuzen Bowls, was founded on the island of Kyushu, Japan over 50 years ago by Mr. Takaharu Shigemitsu. Every bowl of ramen we serve is a masterpiece, crafted with time-honored techniques, premium ingredients, and a deep respect for Japanese culinary traditions. At Ryuzen Bowls, we don’t just serve ramen—we serve a story of culture, flavor, and dedication. Step in, slurp loud, and savor the soul of Japan in every bite.

What Makes Ryuzen Bowls Ramen Unforgettable

Rich, Flavorful Broth

Simmered for hours, our broths are packed with deep, authentic flavors that define every bowl.

Well-Balanced Toppings

From tender chashu to marinated eggs, every topping adds texture and enhances the flavor.

Perfectly Cooked Noodles

Springy, chewy, and cooked just right, our noodles are crafted to complement the broth perfectly.

Presentation and Passion

Every bowl is beautifully arranged, reflecting our care and passion for authentic ramen.

Our Ramen

Tonkotsu Ramen

A creamy, rich pork bone broth that's simmered for hours, served with thin noodles, tender chashu pork, soft-boiled eggs, and green onions. Perfect for those who love a bold, hearty flavor.

Shoyu Ramen

A soy sauce-based ramen with a clear, savory broth. It's light yet flavorful, topped with bamboo shoots, nori, sliced pork, and a boiled egg. A classic choice for any ramen lover.

Seafood Miso Ramen

A rich miso-based broth paired with seafood like shrimp, scallops, squid, and clams. Topped with bean sprouts, green onions, and a drizzle of sesame oil for a satisfying umami-packed experience.

Shio Ramen

A delicate and clear salt-based broth, offering a light, refreshing taste. Common toppings include chicken, seaweed, pickled plums, and green onions, making it a great option for a milder flavor profile.
